    Meet Dr. Kevin McAllister
    Dr. Kevin McAllister, attended the University of Georgia for his undergraduate and veterinary degrees, with a concentration on small animal medicine and surgery.

    Dr. Kevin McAllister excels in diagnosing intricate veterinary cases, earning a reputation for his remarkable ability to identify challenging conditions.
